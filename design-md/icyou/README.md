# ICYOU

A contemporary clothing brand blending Asian aesthetics with modern streetwear. Inspired by the minimalist beauty of East Asian fashion, we create relaxed, flowing silhouettes that celebrate freedom of movement and self-expression.

## Philosophy

Freedom in every thread. Our designs embrace the body, not constrain it. Inspired by Japanese and Korean street style, we create clothing that feels like a second skin—effortless, timeless, and unmistakably you.

## Collection

Oversized tees, wide-leg trousers, flowy shirts, and relaxed outerwear. Each piece is designed with intentional draping and sustainable materials.

## Brand Values

- **Freedom** — Clothing that moves with you
- **Simplicity** — Clean lines, minimal details
- **Quality** — Sustainable materials, ethical production
- **Expression** — Wear your attitude
